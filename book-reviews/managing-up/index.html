This book feels like it will easily work its way into my shelf of books I'm likely to reference again. While most of the situations aren't currently applicable to me, I think I've experienced most of what the author described - and could have benefitted from some pointers while going through those things. There are repeated "Steal These Scripts" sections throughout to give some samples of things to say in a variety of situations. The author makes a great case for not just building your case as an employee, but maintaining your case as an employee. The subtitle goal to "get what you need from the people in charge" is only part of it, since it also feels like the book aims to help you manage your career and manage your relationship with management consciously and effectively.

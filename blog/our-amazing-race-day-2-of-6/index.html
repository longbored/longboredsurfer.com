Our Amazing Race - Day 2 of 6

Published on 2005-03-18 by Pedro

I attempt to deliver my answer to my father. He has gone MIA and found himself on a golf course and not answering phone calls until after the round.

Sarah calls me twice to find out my answer as well as to shoot around ideas and itineraries. Debate happens as to whether or not a video is required. Alec is left out of the debate, and shoots out an idea for a video. Communication continues to happen throughout the day, and the results settle on this: Alec and I will travel on Saturday to Denver and wait for my father to show up there. We'll sleep there in a quiet room, get up the next day for a 6 AM flight to Washington Dulles. From there, we'll meet up with my uncle and drive up to Philadelphia, and be there at around 4-ish.

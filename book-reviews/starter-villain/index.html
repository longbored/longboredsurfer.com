Starter Villain

Published on 1970-01-01 by John Scalzi

More like three and half out of five. While this book is perfectly enjoyable, with plenty of laugh-out-loud moments, and fun and engaging events, at times it tested my ability to suspend disbelief. Plus, sometimes the language was a bit much. But at the end of the day, I would happily dive right into a sequel if it were available.

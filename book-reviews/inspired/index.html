I read this for work. I liked the first half of this book. It did a great job of enlightening me to a variety of improvements that could be made in my own personal approach to product management as well as some ways I might be able to guide others. The second half of this book got too deep into testing and interacting with users. If I were a product manager (and not just one who is asked to participate in it), maybe I would have liked this book more. My last comment will be about the chapter sizes in this book - varying from one paragraph to multiple pages, they felt comical.

I reread the series yet again, to accompany my second oldest, who is reading them for the first time. At this point, I'm not sure this if my favorite book of the series, but it's really well done. JK Rowling builds to the details and events of this book so thoroughly for the prior 6 books that it's hard to not find enjoyment in seeing it all come together. They're not perfect books, but they're pretty stinkin' good.

The Fifth Season

Read on 2021-08-27

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (15 hours 27 minutes)

This book had engaging storylines, unique points of view, an interesting world, and a slightly different magic system. All of those elements helped bring me into the story, and go through this book rather quickly. I have already happily started the second book.

I'll share my usual points of contention, however: unnecessary (though admittedly not super graphic) sex, and seemingly out of place and excessive vulgarity. You've invented cuss words for your world? Okay. They start saying F this and that? Wait - so, why bother inventing new ones, too?

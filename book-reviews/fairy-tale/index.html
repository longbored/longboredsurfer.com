King seemed to spend a third of the book building a detailed world, which he then abandoned. The fantasy world he also built was rich, and involved. Some of the parts of the story dragged a bit, but unlike some of his other books, there wasn't an overly graphic sex scene. I could see any number of books similarly inhabiting this same world - either the real world, or fantasy world he built.

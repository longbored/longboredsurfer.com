Term Limits

Read on 2009-04-09

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (15 hours 30 minutes)

Although not technically part of the Mitch Rapp series, this book has 90% of the same characters, and takes place chronologically before the Mitch Rapp series. I really enjoyed this installment, and found it to have some great background information for the rest of the series. Part of me wishes I had read it before I had read any of the others.

Feelings about 2006

Published on 2006-12-20 by Pedro

Despite the fact that I publish things on the internet and allow pretty much anybody to read it, I'm still a fairly private person. This year is about to come to a close and I've experienced far more than I've talked about on this blog.

In order to satisfy both my desire to share with you and my tendency towards privacy, I've come up with a solution. I'd like to share with you some of the feelings, thoughts, descriptions, and whatnot that I might use to describe various experiences and times that I've had throughout the year. Take this for what it's worth: (in no particular order)

hope, joy, anxiety, fear, ignorance, sorrow, uncertainty, happy, sadness, relaxed, rested, embarrassment, discontent, shame, relief, strength, humor, pleasure, delighted, boredom, misunderstanding, displeasure, challenged, questioned, overloaded, burdened, awestruck, gratitude, cheated, shortchanged, guilty, tested, loved, admiration, jealousy, confusion, wonder, amazement, enlightened, curiosity, satisfaction

I'm sure there are others, but that's a decent start. Despite the hard times in 2006, it was a very beneficial year and look forward to 2007.

Sorry it's taken me so long to post stuff. There's been a lot going on, and I haven't made this a priority.

Page Copies & Rips

I find stuff that I like, or want to print, or want to keep a copy for myself. Some documents just aren't formatted very well for printing. Being semi-conscious of wasting paper, I end up reformatting them so I can print them off in a better fashion. Since I already do it for me, I feel it's no harm in sharing with others. Credit (hopefully) given where credit is due on each of these. If you're an author and feel that I need more information about these articles, or don't want me to post something, just let me know. Items shared here aren't necessarily representative of me or my opinions, but maybe they are.

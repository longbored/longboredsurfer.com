For We Are Many

Published on 1970-01-01 by Dennis E. Taylor

These books feel so short, yet filled with entertainment. Despite so many similar elements (with the main character able to clone himself), the story is easy to follow. Fortunately, I enjoy the main character, and his various permutations. If you're looking for humorous, easy to ready sci-fi, look no further.

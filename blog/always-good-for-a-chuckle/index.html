Always Good for a Chuckle

Published on 2007-01-31 by Pedro

Every now and again I randomly chuckle to myself that Martha Stewart is an ex-convict. She can't vote, and had to wear a device to make sure she didn't leave her house. Sinner. I stop laughing soon after though, because I remember that she's a billionaire... not that money is everything, but it sure helps.

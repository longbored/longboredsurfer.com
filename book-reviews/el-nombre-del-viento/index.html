El nombre del viento

Read on 2019-07-08

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (27 hours 20 minutes)

  • Previously: The Name of the Wind
  • Next: Why We Sleep

I tackled "El Nombre del Viento" in Spanish this time. This a good book, no matter what language you choose to enjoy it in. I listened to this book in an effort to somewhat brush-up on the elements of Spanish I don't get to use frequently. Some of the Rothfuss' wordplay is lost in translation to Spanish. Despite that, some of his wording feels extremely at-home in the Spanish language. At this point, however, if I get to choose, I'll go back to English for this one.

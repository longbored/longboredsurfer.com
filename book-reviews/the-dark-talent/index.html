The Dark Talent

Read on 2018-09-07

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (5 hours 45 minutes)

This book somehow makes the world, and characters go even deeper than the others. I think what I enjoy in fiction (maybe it's unique to fantasy, maybe it's unique to Brandon Sanderson) is the ability to do more with the story than I can really anticipate. This book proves to be no exception to that. Seriously though, don't bother with this book if you haven't read the others.

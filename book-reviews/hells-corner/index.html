Hell's Corner

Published on 1970-01-01 by David Baldacci

An interesting book. I'm not sure if I like the style I've noticed in this novel (which is also apparent in others). The author likes to put so many twists and turns that you have no chance of guessing what will happen next. Although it keeps you guessing, it seems too convenient, and impossible at the same time. It's a shame, because he's developed some good characters.

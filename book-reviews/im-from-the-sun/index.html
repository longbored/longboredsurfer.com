I'm From the Sun

Read on 2019-05-09

⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (4 hours 10 minutes)

The story itself was somewhat skippable. Not great. Not awful. My kids would probably enjoy it. The real star here is the audio production. Sound effects, voices and talking in the background, the main character breaking into song. If I were rating the book, I'd give it a 2. If I were rating purely the audio, I'd give it a 5. So... 3.

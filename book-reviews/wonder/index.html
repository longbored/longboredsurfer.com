Wonder

Published on 1970-01-01 by R.J. Palacio

A heartwarming story of August; his perspective, resilience, and personality on strong display. Much like in The Help, I wish this book was a work of non-fiction, which I think would lend it more credit. Despite that, for the target audience (of youth and YA), I feel like this is a fantastic book, and a must-read for that age group. For adults, it lacks a bit in story and climax. 4.5 stars?

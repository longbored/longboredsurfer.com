Abraham Lincoln: Vampire Hunter

Published on 1970-01-01 by Seth Grahame-Smith

Enjoyable, but skippable. If you're into vampires getting clobbered by an American President (possibly the greatest ever) throughout his life, this is for you. I honestly struggled the entire time with suspension of disbelief. If I could, I'd give it 2.5 stars. I rounded up for fun.

The Gray Man

Read on 2022-05-28 by Pedro

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Implausible, conveniently coincidental, and the perfect setup for a lengthy series of stereotypical and and highly entertaining spy/thriller novels. If you've enjoyed these types of books/series, this will be right up your alley. I rifled through this thing in about three days.

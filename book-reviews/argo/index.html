Argo

Read on 2013-06-17

⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (9 hours 27 minutes)

I've read (or listened to) lots of spy novels, but the enjoyable detail of this book, was that it was non-fiction. The ending of the story is obvious, since it's part of the title of the book, but how they get there is interesting. Although I wish there were more details about the actual extraction, I really liked all of the side stories told by Mendez.

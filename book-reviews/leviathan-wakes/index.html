Leviathan Wakes

Published on 1970-01-01 by James S.A. Corey

With Mars, and outer planets now within reach, humanity has expanded to occupy the majority of our solar system. Add in some inter-planetary politics, strifes, and human elements, with an additional touch of extra-terrestrial interference, and you've got people similarly grasping for (and abusing) power. That an oversimplification of a book I rather enjoyed.

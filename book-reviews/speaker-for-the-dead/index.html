Speaker for the Dead

Published on 1970-01-01 by Orson Scott Card

An interesting book. Admittedly it isn't very much like Ender's Game, despite having the same main character. What I probably found the most entertainment in, was saying "Speaker for the Dead" in the same way as one might say/sing "Leader of the Pack".

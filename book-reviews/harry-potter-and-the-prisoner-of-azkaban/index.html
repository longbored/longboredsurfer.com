This reading was with/to my youngest. He's an active kid, and shifts positions around the room throughout my reading, doing various fidget activities, but also clearly paying attention to the book. The last third of this book is filled with twists, turns, and exciting action that he had trouble staying still, and was frequently disappointed with the end of our nightly reading. I remember reading it the first time, and struggling to keep up with the events, yet loving the details. Reading it again, it's easy to admire the clues as the book progresses.

No Country for Old Men

Published on 1970-01-01 by Cormac McCarthy

Maybe I missed something, but I didn't really see the conclusion to this book. Overall though, it was fine, if a bit gruesome. Drug deals, death, destruction, and a couple coin tosses. This is the second book I've read by Cormac McCarthy, and neither book have I loved.

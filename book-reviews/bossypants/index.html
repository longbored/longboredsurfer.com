Bossypants

Read on 2020-02-06

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (5 hours 32 minutes)

This is funny, though a bit colorful at times. Memoir? No. Humorous stories and anecdotes? Absolutely. The audio book is read by Tina Fey herself, and if you like Tina Fey, you'll likely enjoy both her book, as well as her reading of it.

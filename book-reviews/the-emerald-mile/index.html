The Emerald Mile

Published on 1970-01-01 by Kevin Fedarko

I couldn't have cared less about this topic before reading this book. After reading this, I would LOVE to spend however long is needed to take a wooden dory through the Grand Canyon, reveling in the beauty of nature, and enjoying the rich history of the canyon. A fantastic book.

Another great example of why it's good to be in a book club.

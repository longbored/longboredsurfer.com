Unbroken

Read on 2012-09-07 by Pedro

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

What an inspirational and fascinating story. My heart was/is saddened by the atrocities committed, but is gladdened at the resilience, optimism, and forgiving nature of mankind. I particularly enjoyed the inventiveness and creativity in the subversive activities of the POWs. Louis Zamperini, here's to you, the men of the Green Hornet, POWs, and to those who fought/fight for us.

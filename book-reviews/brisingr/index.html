Brisingr

Published on 1970-01-01 by Christopher Paolini

Probably the best book of the series so far. Any of my qualms with the two previous books (lack of character development, and in general a rushed feeling) were handled by the author, who has obviously matured a bit since writing Eragon/Eldest. Looking forward to the final book.

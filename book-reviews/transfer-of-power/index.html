Transfer of Power

Read on 2008-11-20 by Pedro

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

It's been a while since I've read any espionage books, but this book reminded me why I enjoy them. Although some authors have chosen to write books less often (*cough*Tom Clancy*cough*), Vince Flynn appears to be taking over the helm. I'm looking forward to the other books in this series. Truly an enjoyable book.

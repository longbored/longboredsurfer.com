Peter and the Starcatchers

Read on 2021-05-15

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (8 hours 40 minutes)

This was a fun and clever spin on a classic story. Peter and a group of fellow orphans, start on a trip on the boat "Neverland". I know this is aimed at a much younger audience, but I found myself easily enjoying the story, amused by the wit of weaving the familiar aspects together in a new way, and laughing out loud at "the sisters". Good times.

What a shame this book only takes about two hours to read. It's a unique story, and a really enjoyable setup. Too bad it seems that this short novella is a one-time book that probably won't see any additional story-telling (at least by Sanderson).

Stephen Leeds, a man who has hallucinations of a dozens of people (all experts in various fields) is tasked with finding a man who stole a camera - a camera that can take pictures of the past.

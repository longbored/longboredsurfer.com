"This Tender Land"

by William Kent Krueger

  • Read on 2026-02-04
  • Rating: ️️️️️
  • Format: 🎧 (14 hours 19 minutes)

The tale of Odie, Albert, Mose and Emmy as they flee a bad situation after a series of bad events. It's an enjoyable story, but left me feeling uneasy frequently. I enjoyed the descriptions, and felt like I was experiencing much of what was happening. The book also does a good job of showing more light on the situations of a variety of people. US History is littered with bad decisions, and people left in the wake of those decisions.

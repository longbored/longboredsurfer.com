Divine Justice

Published on 1970-01-01 by David Baldacci

I finished this in 3 days, which should speak for itself in a review. This book stepped further away from the espionage, and more towards the domestic troubles - but I'd prefer the former style. Unrelated: I'm impressed by the clever naming of the books.

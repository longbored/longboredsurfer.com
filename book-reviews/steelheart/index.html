Steelheart

Published on 1970-01-01 by Brandon Sanderson

I really liked the world that Brandon Sanderson built for this - the idea that superheroes/Epics exist due to some single incident. At the same time, it seems that the Epics are inherently bad. Together with bad metaphors, this group is trying to bring sanity and happiness back to this world.

"James"

by Percival Everett

  • Read on 2026-03-11
  • Rating: ️️️️️
  • Format: 🎧 (7 hours 49 minutes)

This was excellent. Jim of Huckleberry Finn origin, tells his side of the story, as James. The core of the story won't be astounding to anybody familiar with The Adventures of Huckleberry Finn, but the presentation is top notch. Percival Everett, the author, tells a great story, and makes Jim/James even more likable than before. The "peculiar institution" was an abomination, and I love that modern voices get to help deepen our understanding that, and see the humanity in others.

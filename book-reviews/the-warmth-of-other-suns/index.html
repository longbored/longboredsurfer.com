The Warmth of Other Suns

Read on 2021-02-08

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (22 hours 40 minutes)

  • Previously: Rhythm of War
  • Next: Dances with Wolves

I really liked this one. Admittedly, I'm not sure I paid much attention to the Great Migration before, though I long saw the reasons why I would have left. This book helped further illuminate the trials of those who made (or didn't make) the journey, and the issues of a new environment, and the continued struggles of people in their new homes within this great and evolving nation.

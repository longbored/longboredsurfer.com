Artemis Fowl

Published on 1970-01-01 by Eoin Colfer

An enjoyable book, and fabulous voice acting. Obviously you'll only experience the latter if you're able to listen to the book. I'm anxious to start the next book, to see how the series continues... a simple story of a genius boy and his criminal endeavors.

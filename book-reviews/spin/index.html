Spin

Read on 2012-04-16 by Pedro

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

What an enjoyable book this was, with great mind candy. I like books that mess with your mind a little bit (but not too much, since I have enough to think about as-is). I'm curious to see how the following two books in the trilogy will play out (especially given how small/short they are in comparison to this first one).

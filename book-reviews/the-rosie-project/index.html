The Rosie Project

Published on 1970-01-01 by Graeme Simsion

I had several good laughs throughout this book. Don Tillman's lengthy questionnaire to find a suitable mate drew me right in. I couldn't argue with his logic, but laughed at the obvious awkwardness of situations and encounters. Bring an anatomy skeleton, daphne flowers, martial arts skills, and get ready for a funny story.

"Murder Your Employer: The McMasters Guide to Homicide"

by Rupert Holmes

  • Read on 2026-03-27
  • Rating: ️️️️️
  • Format: 🎧 (14 hours 13 minutes)

This was a humorously shared story of people being trained in a sophisticated way to become "delete-ists". (We don't like the M word here, despite its use to start the name of the book.) The story mainly follows a character in his efforts to make his world a better place, and ends up in an educational institution specializing in this craft (not in a serial way). The amount of death/killing puns was off the charts. The storylines were engaging. An easy book to enjoy - and a pleasant surprise for a book club.

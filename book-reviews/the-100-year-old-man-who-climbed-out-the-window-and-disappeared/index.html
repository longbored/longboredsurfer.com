The 100-Year-Old Man Who Climbed Out the Window and Disappeared

Published on 1970-01-01 by Jonas Jonasson

A Swedish combo of Forrest Gump and The Power of One. Obviously implausible, yet entertaining. Like other stories, I enjoyed the weaving in of the protagonist's past while telling the present story. Good times.

The Adventures of Tom Sawyer

Published on 1970-01-01 by Mark Twain

Though revered by others, Tom is generally a twit. This felt too episodic. That said, there are so many classic moments from the book, it's hard to rate it too poorly. The amount of superstition shown by these boys is comical, but also not completely dissimilar to how I thought of things as a young boy.

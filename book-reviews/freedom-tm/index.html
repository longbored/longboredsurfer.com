Freedom (TM)

Published on 1970-01-01 by Daniel Suarez

The inevitable follow-up to Daniel Suarez's Daemon. The action picks right up where Daemon left off. Honestly, I don't know how you could read Daemon, like it, and _not_ read this book. The conflict between the democracy and meritocracy increases. Although this book is a bit more preachy than the previous, the action is still just as enjoyable. There are enough twists and turns (in what originally felt like would be a highly predictable book), to keep you second guessing, and engaged. Despite this, some of the themes get overplayed, and ultimately I wonder if I'd read a third book in the series.

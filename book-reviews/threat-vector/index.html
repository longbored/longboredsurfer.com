Threat Vector

Published on 1970-01-01 by Tom Clancy

Another fun book in the Jack Ryan Jr. series. China is at it again, but now with cyber espionage in the mix. With electronics being suspect, what I enjoyed was the reintroduction of more of the classic, old-school espionage tricks. Sure the book was/is predictable, but it keeps your attention as you try to figure out how they're going to get to what you know will end up happening.

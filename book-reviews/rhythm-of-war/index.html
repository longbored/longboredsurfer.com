Rhythm of War

Published on 1970-01-01 by Brandon Sanderson

I really liked this book. Brandon Sanderson continues to be one of my favorite authors as he interweaves various storylines, and welcomes you into a world that is both complete fantasy, and totally relatable all at the same time.

Like so many other series, if you haven't read the prior books, I don't think it's for you. The pacing was much better than the third book, and the storylines were much more engaging. I loved the various developments of the story, and feel somehow much more engaged in the series than I was before. Lastly, I appreciate the openness and delicateness he approaches mental health in this book. It's far more common than some would have you believe, and it's nothing to be ashamed of.

Second Review: On my second time through this book, agree with my initial review. Admittedly, this was the single book I decided to revisit prior to starting the next book. This book started with a few unfamiliar elements, but I appreciated how the author would do some gentle reminders. I'm thoroughly excited for the next book in this series.

Ender's Shadow

Published on 1970-01-01 by Orson Scott Card

Having read Ender's Game for the first time a little over a year ago (and rereading it two more times since), I really liked this book. What a great, fresh perspective, and a fantastic idea of telling a famous story from the point of view of somebody else. Now the debate continues if I ever bother seeing the movie.

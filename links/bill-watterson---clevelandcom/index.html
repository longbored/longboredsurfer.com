Bill Watterson - Cleveland.com

Published on 1970-01-01 by

Cleveland.com gets a rare interview with the creator of Calvin \& Hobbes - one of my favorite comic strips. His answers aren't very long, and he comes off a little harsh, but that has an added touch of charm to a creative genius like him. Still, interesting to read.

"I think some of the reason 'Calvin and Hobbes' still finds an audience today is because I chose not to run the wheels off it."

Cinder

Published on 1970-01-01 by Marissa Meyer

This is my eldest's favorite series, so I wanted to try it. Although the writing is clearly aimed for a YA audience, it has a very engaging and richly imaginative world. It takes a Cinderella-based story, and implement a version of it in a futuristic world, with cyborgs, androids, a lunar-based people, a plague, and much more. Admittedly, I'm totally sucked in, ready for the next book.

What a fun book. I thoroughly enjoyed vicariously experiencing life on a South African wildlife preserve. Lawrence Anthony very clearly states that he's not the elephant whisperer, but that elephants are the whisperers. He relates experiences while running this preserve - centrally told around the initial complications, but continued relationship with these largest of land mammals. These otherwise terrifyingly strong, protective, and curious animals seem fully capable of being gentle giants.

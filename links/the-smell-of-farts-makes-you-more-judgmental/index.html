The Smell of Farts Makes you more Judgmental

Published on 2009-04-24 by Pedro

Although I don't think there's room for any guessing in the question at hand, I think the results are interesting. If you're asked to make a moral judgement while smelling a fart, chances are you'll tend to be more moral or judgmental than if everything is smelling like roses.

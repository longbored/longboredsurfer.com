Beneath a Scarlet Sky

Published on 1970-01-01 by Mark T. Sullivan

Remarkable. The author prefaces the story saying it's based on actual events, but has added and embellished things. Although I'd like to know which things deviated, or weren't true, I'm not going to let that stop me from enjoying the story. Parts of the story were interesting perspectives to consider. Others were a stark reminder of the brutalities of WWII.

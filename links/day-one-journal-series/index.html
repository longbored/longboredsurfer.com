Day One: Journal Series

Published on 2014-02-17 by Pedro

I love Day One (iPhone/iPad / OS X). Writing in a journal has been basically ground-breaking for me (mentally, emotionally, spiritually, and probably in more ways). Here, Tulio Jarocki gives some great tips and insight into the process, and is certainly worth a read if you're thinking about getting started - even if it's not with Day One. If it's not Day One, I have to recommend some Field Notes notebooks, which are my second addiction.

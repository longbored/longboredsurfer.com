The Gathering Storm

Published on 1970-01-01 by Robert Jordan

I'm not sure if it was the anticipation of the book, or the completion/continuation of all the story-lines and pending issues, or the actual quality of writing... or a combination of those three, but I *really* enjoyed this book. What a great series. I eagerly look forward to the next installment. "Each time we live we get to love again."

Despite the truly scarring imagery, I'd recommend this book to most. A startling view of what could be.

The book continued to make me jumpy, probably because part of me could see the storyline as a possible taste of the future. Interesting writing style - never knowing exactly when somebody was speaking or not, and lots of short sentences or fragments. It made for an uneasy feeling.

This reading was with my youngest. For a long time this was my favorite book of the series. Part of me was concerned that the books were falling into a similar pattern, and this one takes a great turn in, what feels like, a reasonable way. My son was completely captivated by the challenges, and then completely thrown off by the maze-like twists and turns in how the book concludes. So good.

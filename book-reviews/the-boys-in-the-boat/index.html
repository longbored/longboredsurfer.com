The Boys in the Boat

Read on 2017-12-12

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (14 hours 24 minutes)

I need to go watch a boat race, and you need to read this book. It probably won't change your life, but it'll surely give you warm-fuzzies about a group of young men who accomplished something rather incredible during a tricky time in American (and world) history. Great (hi)story. Well told.

"Heaven's River"

by Dennis E. Taylor

  • Read on 2026-04-25
  • Rating: ️️️️️
  • Format: 🎧 (16 hours 57 minutes)

Another entertaining book in the series. This one is largely focused on some otter-like creatures, and the attempted rescue of a long-lost friend.

Sometimes the storylines are hard to keep track of (or at least the introductory names of the various lines). But the environments are rich with description. The science (although fiction) feels understandable. Most importantly to me: I'm amused by the characters, and laugh out loud at various points in the books.

Executive Power

Published on 1970-01-01 by Vince Flynn

Although enjoyable, the author seemed to be trying to tackle two semi-unrelated storylines in the same book. I know each book is a fairly thin and quick read, but it'd be nice to see a more developed plot in each (not to mention the characters as well).

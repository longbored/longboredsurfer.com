The Help

Published on 1970-01-01 by Kathryn Stockett

One of the better books I've read or listened to in quite a while. Great characters, good topic, well written, and (for those of you who actually might ever listen to it) a fabulous audio performance. I almost couldn't stop laughing out loud after the two slice Hilly part.

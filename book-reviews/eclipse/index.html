Eclipse

Published on 1970-01-01 by Stephenie Meyer

A better installment, though I could summarize the entire book in one sentence. I find I enjoy the Cullen storyline more than the Black storyline, since those characters are much more unique and captivating. I've been told the fourth book is the best of the series, so I look forward to that.

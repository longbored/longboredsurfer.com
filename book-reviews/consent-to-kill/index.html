Consent to Kill

Read on 2009-02-13

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (17 hours 12 minutes)

  • Previously: Memorial Day
  • Next: Act of Treason

My request from the previous book was fulfilled in this book, and Mitch stepped up to maintain his qualifications as a true bad boy. Of all the books in this series, this one reminds me most of Tom Clancy's Without Remorse book. A truly enjoyable book and re-energizing for the remainder of the series (at least the three that are written to-date).

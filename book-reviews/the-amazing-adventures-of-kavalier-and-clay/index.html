The Amazing Adventures of Kavalier & Clay

Read on 2018-08-06

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (26 hours 20 minutes)

  • Previously: The Warded Man
  • Next: Age of Myth

Entertaining in a new and different way. In theory, I really like comic books - they tell stories in a unique way that I can't find anywhere else. In practice, I never read comics. Regardless, this book tells of some comic book creators, their lives and struggles in the build-up, midst of, and life after WWII. It's a unique story that helped me gain some perspective and appreciation. Well done.

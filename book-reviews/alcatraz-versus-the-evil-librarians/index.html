Alcatraz Versus the Evil Librarians

Read on 2011-02-05

⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: physical ()

I thoroughly enjoyed this book (though my rating wouldn't show it). I liked the playful style and informal feel of the book. I imagine it would be perfect for the target audience (teens), and for that alone, I need to rate it lower for adults. Lots of unknowns and it felt a bit rushed. Despite that, it's a great Sanderson book.

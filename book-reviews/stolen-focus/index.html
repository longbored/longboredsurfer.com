"Stolen Focus: Why You Can't Pay Attention--and How to Think Deeply Again"

by Johann Hari

  • Read on 2026-04-21
  • Rating: ️️️️️
  • Format: 🎧 (10 hours 21 minutes)

This isn't a self-help book, in that the author won't talk you through how he personally solved the problem and give you steps to follow to similarly address the problem within you. But he does talk about some of the causes. And some of the causes and their histories are interesting. While he advocates for some self-mastery, he takes a broader step to say you're not entirely to blame for attention issues, and advocates for broad efforts (and legislation) to help reign in some of the technological influences that have taken such a firm grasp on so many.

Really interesting overall all. It was heavy on some of the doom and gloom aspects of this. And I wish more effort would have been spent on the second half of the subtitle, instead of pushing so hard on broad legislation.

The Desert Spear

Published on 1970-01-01 by Peter V. Brett

A good second installment in the series. It's interesting the get the background and perspective of Jardir. I like that the author has brought back numerous elements of the first book for further explanation and detail, yet clearly moved the overall storyline along. The one piece _really_ I don't care for, however, is the author's use of excessive sexual details, and rape. Taking a full star off for that alone.

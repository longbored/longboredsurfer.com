The Body

Read on 2020-08-18

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (14 hours 4 minutes)

Bill Bryson is somehow both thoroughly detailed, yet so brief as to leave me wanting more. I learned so many tidbits from this book (and was fascinated by so many of them), it's hard for me to recall any single one of them while I write this. I anticipate that for quite some time I'll spout off random facts or insights about the body, as things naturally come up in conversation.

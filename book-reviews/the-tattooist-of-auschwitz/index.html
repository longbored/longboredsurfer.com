The Tattooist of Auschwitz

Read on 2022-09-30

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (7 hours 25 minutes)

The atrocities of the concentration camps and death camps are hard to imagine. While this is based on historical events, obviously there are some indulgences to help with the flow of the story. And I loved the story. I can only hope there were many more glimmers of hope among the people in the camps as they attempted to endure their journey.

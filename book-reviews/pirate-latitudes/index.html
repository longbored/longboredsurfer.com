Pirate Latitudes

Published on 1970-01-01 by Michael Crichton

This is a bit unlike Crichton's usual science-fiction style books, but still an entertaining book. I'll certainly miss his work. Cancer sucks. Some of the imagery is not for the faint of heart (but that makes sense, considering it's about sailors/pirates/privateers in the 1600's).

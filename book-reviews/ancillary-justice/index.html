In a society with anything from machine to human, and seemingly every combination of the two in between, and a supreme and total ruler able to inhabit thousands of bodies simultaneously, the seeming total control by one society seems all-seeing. And then there's Breq.

Pure science-fiction. It's been a while since I've read a sci-fi novel, and while this one felt a little slow to start, the author has built a really cool world/galaxy/universe. I'm a sucker for the approach of telling the back story progressively while also telling the current story, which happens here. And when the two storylines meet, it always feels revelatory for me.

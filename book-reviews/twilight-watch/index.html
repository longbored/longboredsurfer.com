Twilight Watch

Published on 1970-01-01 by Sergei Lukyanenko

I liked this book much better than #2/Night Watch. Although the novelty of the series was strongest in the first book, I felt like this book returned to the roots of what may have attracted people to the series to begin with. Overall I'd give the series as a whole 3 stars.

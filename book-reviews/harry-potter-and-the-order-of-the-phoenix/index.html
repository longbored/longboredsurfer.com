"Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix"

by J.K. Rowling · Harry Potter #4

  • Rating: ️️️️️

I read this with my youngest. Obviously, I take umbrage with Umbridge. She's written to be an annoying character, and performs her role well. This time while reading the book, while my son was bouncing around the room in his excitement and frustration with the events and characters, I noticed more of the foreshadowing and bridging this book does. Plus, Harry didn't come across as whiny as he had been on prior readings of this book. A great installment in the series.

The Best Gifts

Read on 2024-09-03 by Pedro

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

A religious book, focusing on gifts of the spirit, and how not only should we seek after them, and develop what we have, but we likely have gifts not explicitly spelled out in the scriptures. This was a rather short book, and could use some contemplation after some of the chapters. I loved the examples from The Book of Mormon (and admit to using two of them in recent lessons).

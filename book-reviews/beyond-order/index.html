"Beyond Order: 12 More Rules for Life"

by Jordan B. Peterson

  • Read on 2026-06-15
  • Rating: ️️️️️
  • Format: 🎧 (13 hours 11 minutes)

I read this author's prior book, but am otherwise unfamiliar with his work. Much like his last book, Peterson takes a very logical approach to each "rule", and that logic can be hard to pick up if you've lost the thread. Some points felt like leaps. Some points were belabored. I don't agree with all of his "rules" (which doesn't make the others invalid), but I thoroughly appreciate his logic. I think he has an interesting perspective, and can appreciate how he has arrived at each rule.

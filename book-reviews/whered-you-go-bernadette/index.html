Where'd You Go, Bernadette

Read on 2019-05-18 by Pedro

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

This was a fun one. The story is told through a collection of correspondance, as you see the various sides of the story. The drama in my life is nothing like what these people experience/generate (or maybe it is, and I'm simply ignoring it or unaware of it), and I found it highly entertaining. Bernadette - you're okay in my book.

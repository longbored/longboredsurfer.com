The Alloy of Law

Read on 2012-01-01

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (10 hours 48 minutes)

  • Previously: Warbreaker (Graphic Audio Version)
  • Next: The Civil War, Vol. 1

I really enjoyed this book. I wouldn't recommend you read it, if you haven't read any of Brandon Sanderson's Mistborn trilogy. Although this book doesn't overlap with that series, it takes place in the same world (just a few hundred years later). His semi-modernization of that world is brilliant, and I felt a connection with the characters immediately. My only complaint is that the book was so short. I certainly look forward to the other two in this second part of his trilogy of trilogies.

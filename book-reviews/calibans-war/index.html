Caliban’s War

Published on 1970-01-01 by James S.A. Corey

After the first book in this series, I thought I knew what I was in for. I was wrong, and pleasantly surprised by that. Although the overarching themes and messages are widely applicable, this book is solid sci-fi, so if that's not your thing, don't bother.

Zero G

Published on 1970-01-01 by Dan Wells

Maybe it's simple 4 hour books like this that help me remember how much I enjoy Science Fiction. It helps with my suspension of disbelief. Quick story. Certainly manageable by kids. Interesting premise. Highly enjoyable. I'll probably need to look at more by Dan Wells.

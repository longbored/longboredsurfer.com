Simply put - this book is fantastic. Others have said it, and I agree - this should be required reading for everyone. Maybe it'll give you some perspective. Maybe not. I want my kids to read this. I want my wife to read this. I want my friends to read this. I want my neighbors to read this. I want people to talk about this book, and do something. Truly, a fantastic book.

Second Review

This was my second time through this book in less than six months. It has a lot of cussing, but it's a great story, providing a needed perspective. If you haven't read this book, you should.

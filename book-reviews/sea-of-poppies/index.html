Sea of Poppies

Published on 1970-01-01 by Amitav Ghosh

Although I liked the cultural elements of this book, I was ultimately underwhelmed by the book's progress. It felt like 500+ pages of building characters, sharing wildly different back-stories. Then... nothing. If this was all a build-up to the actual story in book two, I fear Ghosh has already lost my attention.

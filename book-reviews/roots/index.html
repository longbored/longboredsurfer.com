Roots

Read on 2018-05-07

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Format: audio (30 hours 6 minutes)

The best book I've read in quite a while. Captivating. Heartbreaking. Fantastic.

Very appropriately subtitled "The Saga of an American Family", as the book follows multiple people through a family's history. I'd love to know more, and I can only imagine how much more they wish they knew as well.

A Wrinkle in Time

Read on 2019-12-13 by Pedro

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

I had never read this before. I wonder what a book like this would have done for me as a young reader - potentially igniting my interest in books, and coming to early grips with the fact that I would enjoy (see: love) sci-fi and fantasy novels. Interesting characters, a comfortable overarching theme, various unique elements, and a relatable message. A win.
